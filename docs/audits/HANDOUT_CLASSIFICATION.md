## Material not safe for canonical Perspection entry copy

These phrases or moves conflict with Perspection governance if placed directly on the Perspection entry surface:

- “A Framework for Taking Back Your Story”
- “The Skill: Perspection”
- “Perspection is powered by the Trinity Method”
- “Practice this”
- “first step”
- any phrasing that makes Perspection feel sequential, instructional, recovery-oriented, or progress-based

## Perspection-safe rewrite direction

If any piece of this handout is adapted for Perspection, it should shift from instruction to observation.

Instead of:

> Perspection is the art of seeing clearly in the middle of the fire.

Use a quieter governance-compatible direction such as:

> Perspection names a stance of inspecting perception before fixing meaning.

Instead of:

> Practice this, and you will learn to harness your fire.

Use:

> No motion is required for the observation to remain valid.
